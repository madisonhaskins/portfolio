Searcy Parks and Recreation

Providing a soluton for the Searcy Department Parks and Recreation.

From sports to movies currently playing at the local Rialto theatre, Searcy Parks and Recreation only communicated using their Facebook page. After meeting with the department it was clear they needed a solution that could scale with the growing population of the city. Myself along with my classmates at Harding University created a beautiful web presence for the department to communicate with the community. However we didn’t stop there. The department wanted the site to be completely editable so we created an administrative backdoor that gave the technologically challenged department the ability to easily and intuitively edit the site and it’s images and text. Because of my experience programming with both front-end and back-end languages I took a developer role on the team.


Skills refined:

  1. Front-end web development: HTML, CSS, Javascript
  2. Backend web development: php, Javascript
  3. Teamwork
  4. Taking specific requirements and creating a solution

Walmart Idenity Management Portal

Prototype of a unified portal for Walmart identity management and certification.

With my experience working as a product manager for Walmart identity management I’ve encountered several products that were not designed with user experience in mind. Currently we use to applications to accomplish compliance related tasks. Each one is used quarterly by thousands of users from all over the world. After receiving feedback about user experience quarter over quarter it was decided a new front-end of these applications needed to be made. Because of my involvement in Bloc, I was able to jump on the opportunity andcreate a prototype of a new interface for our user access review program. Because of my extensive knowledge of the feedback from our users and the requirements for overall function I was able to create something that would benefit both our end-users and my team. It just goes to show that an opportunity to improve UI/UX can arise in any job and any department and I am thankful I had the opportunity to allow my job at the time and my passions intersect.
